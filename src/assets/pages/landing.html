Introduction

The whole world talks about the rapidly changing car industry with self-driving cars, innovative car-sharing concepts and, most importantly, electric vehicles (EVs). A new era of mobility has begun - so they say. But has it really?

In reality, the total number of EVs sold is still staggeringly low compared to convential ICE cars - despite of various incentives to buy electric. That is because car manufacturers all over the world were struggling with providing electric cars that are both attractive and affordable for the masses.
